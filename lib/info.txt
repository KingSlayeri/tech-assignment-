The 2022 Audi S4 3.0 TFSI QUATTRO is an all wheel drive 4 Door Sedan that was released to
the Australian market on 1st January 2022 classified as a 8W MY22A.
The Audi S4 is regarded as a medium from
60k built in Germany with prices from a dealer as a used car starting at $98,300.

The Audi S4 is an all wheel drive 4 door with 5 seats,
powered by a 3.0L Turbo V6 engine that has 260 kW of power (at 5400 rpm) and
500 Nm of torque (at 1370 rpm) via a 8 Speed Automatic Tiptronic.
